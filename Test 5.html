At Games Galore we have developed not one, but TWO professional games. The first being a calm, relaxing Roll a Ball game, where the player rolls around a maze, searching for cubes to pick up

Though the Second is far more intruiging. At Games Galore we have developed the next revolutionary First Person Shooter, named "The DOOM Shooter". The Doom Shooter is a simple yet also complex

First Person Shooter that challenges the player. The game contains 9 major levels, and one sublevel. The game has unique maps for each level, 10 Different Guns to utilize, 8 Different Enemies

each with their own different health, damage and type, and 2 Bosses. The game contains many features such as movement, jumping, shooting, aiming, crouching, sprinting and a pause menu.

The game has a checkpoint system and an advanced pause menu that lets you change look sensitivity, toggle fps counter and even enable invincibility (just for a bit more added fun).
